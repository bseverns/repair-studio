# Safety Policy

- **PPE & ESD** available and used where appropriate.
- **Risk Gates**: follow the Green/Yellow/Red system before work (see signage).
- **Lockout/Tagout** for mains power and dangerous machinery.
- No solo high-risk work. Pair or escalate to a qualified lead.
- First-aid kit, spill kit, and fire extinguisher locations are posted.

Emergencies: call local services; then notify hosts.
