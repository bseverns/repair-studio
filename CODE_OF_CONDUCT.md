# Code of Conduct

We commit to a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment. Be kind. Assume good faith. No harassment or discrimination.

- Honor pronouns and names.
- No demeaning language or exclusionary jokes.
- Observe consent-forward policies (see `/policies/consent-policy.md`).

**Reporting:** Use a private channel/email listed in `SECURITY.md`. Include context and links. Reports are handled promptly and confidentially.

Consequences range from a nudge to removal from the space or repository.
