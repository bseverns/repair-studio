# RISK GATES

**GREEN** — reversible, doc-only, low voltage → Go. Log lab_risk_flags anyway.
**YELLOW** — mains-adjacent / structural / live firmware flash / solder near irreplaceables → safety_steward sign-off + PPE check.
**RED** — energized mains, life-safety, unknown chem/bio → Stop & escalate to lab_manager before touching anything.

Locate: PPE • ESD • First-aid • Spill kit • Extinguisher • Lockout tags
